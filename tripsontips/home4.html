North India Tour Packages

Stretching from the mighty Himalayas to the brave Rajput regions and on the other side, from the lush valleys of Kashmir to the pilgrimage rich Uttar Pradesh, North India is a land of enthralling lifestyle and beauty. A holiday in North India will be filled with nature, piety, splendor, rejuvenation and adventure. North India tours are not just the iconic Golden Triangle, Rajasthan and the snow-filled regions. You have more than hundred different styles and combinations of places to visit in the northern part of this peninsular. North India is the most popular tourist destination in the country. The region is filled with holy cities like Amritsar, Varanasi, Rishikesh, Haridwar, Dharamsala and others. You can find numerous cultural regions like Delhi, Gwalior, Khajuraho, Allahabad, Jaipur and others. If you are looking for hill station, choose Mount Abu, Ladakh, Shimla, Darjeeling and others.

The same goes for its list of destinations in numerous genre of holidays like wildlife, art, cuisine, architectural sightseeing and much more. The northern part of the country was ruled by different reigns and emperors in the past few centuries. It was also invaded by Persians and others, who infused their culture into the land. On the eastern side, you can find Tibetan mixed culture, which adds more beauty and diversity to the land. When it comes to North India tour packages, tourists have a large list of options from theme-based vacation like wildlife attractions, Buddhist pilgrimage site and others to region based vacation like Rajput regions, Ladakh, Himalayan regions, Mughal destinations and others. Each style of vacation would be at the prime beauty during a specific time. For instance, the valleys of Uttarkhand, Kashmir and Shimla would be at prime beauty during late summer or early monsoon season. On the other hand, Rajasthan, Delhi and others would also be at prime beauty during winter. When it comes to wildlife attractions, it is better to choose late summers to spot large mammals and winter for a lush vegetation. North India has numerous international airports at Mumbai, Delhi and other destinations, which cover numerous destinations around the world. Thus, entering India is easy, no matter where you are. It is hard to choose the right mixture of destinations inside the country for a beautiful vacation. This is where the North India tour packages come handy. Based on your desire of vacation style, time availability and other factors, you can choose the package for your vacation.
